Self control is at  the root of all virtues.Let a man give rein to his impulses and passions and from that moment he yeilds up his moral
freedom and is carried along the current of life and becomes a slave of his strongest desire.to be morally free to be 
more than animal man must be able to resist the instinctive impulse and this can be done only by the exercise of self control
.thus it is this power which constitutes the real distinction betwwen physical and moral life and form the primary basis of 
an individual character.
